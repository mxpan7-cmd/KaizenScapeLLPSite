Accountant

Bookkeeping, financial statement reviews, and expense management for our clients.

Role TypeFinance & Accounts
Work ModeHybrid
ExperienceFreshers & Experienced
LanguageFluent English — mandatory

We're looking for a sharp, reliable Accountant to support bookkeeping, financial statement reviews, and expense management for our clients.

What You'll Do

  • Maintain accurate day-to-day bookkeeping and general ledger entries
  • Record and categorize expenses and transactions
  • Review and reconcile financial statements on a weekly basis
  • Track and process expense reports; flag anomalies or discrepancies
  • Assist in preparing balance sheets, P&L summaries, and cash flow reports
  • Coordinate with clients via email and calls for document collection and clarifications
  • Maintain organised digital records and filing systems
  • Support audit preparation and respond to finance queries
  • Pay monthly invoices and bills; file monthly sales tax and other reports
  • Coordinate with CPA and QuickBooks on bookkeeping and ledger entries

Who You Are

  • Fluent in English — you can write clear emails and hold professional calls
  • Highly detail-oriented with a strong sense of numerical accuracy
  • Self-driven and comfortable working independently from home
  • Organised and deadline-conscious — you don't let things slip
  • Comfortable with spreadsheets and digital tools (MS Excel / Google Sheets at minimum)

Nice to Have

  • Familiarity with QuickBooks, Xero, Zoho Books, or Tally
  • Prior exposure to bookkeeping for US or international clients
  • Understanding of basic financial reporting concepts
Human Resources Hybrid 2–3 Years Experience

HR Executive

End-to-end recruitment and payroll operations for our clients.

Role TypeHuman Resources
Work ModeHybrid
Experience2–3 Years
EducationCapability over credentials

We're hiring an HR Executive to own recruitment and payroll operations for our clients. Hands-on, client-facing — you'll source and screen candidates, manage the full hiring lifecycle, and handle payroll inputs and processing.

What You'll Do

  • Manage end-to-end recruitment: postings, sourcing, screening, interview scheduling, and offer coordination
  • Communicate with candidates and hiring managers professionally in English
  • Coordinate weekly payroll inputs: attendance, leaves, variable pay, additions and deductions
  • Process payroll accurately and on schedule; generate pay slips and salary registers
  • Handle onboarding documentation and induction coordination for new hires
  • Maintain and update employee records and HR databases
  • Assist with full & final settlement calculations for exiting employees
  • Respond to employee and client HR queries in a timely manner
  • Assist with insurance and payroll audits; maintain employee census securely

Who You Are

  • Fluent in English — strong written and spoken communication is non-negotiable
  • People-oriented with good instincts for evaluating candidates
  • Highly organised and capable of managing multiple open positions simultaneously
  • Reliable and self-managed — you meet deadlines without being chased
  • Comfortable working in a remote, async-friendly environment

Nice to Have

  • Prior experience with payroll tools
  • Familiarity with HR platforms
  • Exposure to recruitment across tech, operations, or professional services roles
  • Understanding of payroll components: CTC structuring, deductions, gross vs net pay
